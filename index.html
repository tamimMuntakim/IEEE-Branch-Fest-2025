About IEEE BranchFest 2025

IEEE BranchFest 2025 is the premier nationwide innovation and science carnival organized by the IEEE BUP Student Branch, WIE Affinity Group, and Computer Society Chapter. This flagship event brings together the brightest minds from academia and industry to showcase talent, foster innovation, and create meaningful connections.
